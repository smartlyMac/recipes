Ingredients:
	- 500g beef mince
	- 400g tin chopped tomatoes
	- 1 onion
	- 3 garlic cloves
	- 2 tbsp tomato puree
	- 1 beef stock cube
	- 2 tbsp Basil
	- 2 tbsp Parsley
	- 1 tbsp Paprika
	- 2 tbsp Worcester Sauce

Directions:
	1. Add small amount of oil to medium saucepan and use a paper towel
	to grease the base and sides. Add the mince and cook on a medium 
	heat until browned

	2. While beef is cooking, add small amount of oil to a frying pan 
	and put on a medium heat. Chop the onion and garlic and fry until 
	lightly coloured

	3. Remove saucepan from the heat and pour off excess fat from the 
	beef. Add the onions and garlic to the beef and mix. 

	4. Add tomatoes, tomato puree, beef stock cube, basil, parsley, 
	paprika, and worcester sauce to the saucepan - DO NOT mix yet

	5. Add water to the saucepan to cover the mince ONLY. Now mix all 
	ingredients together

	6. Put saucepan back on the heat, bring to a boil then reduce heat 
	and simmer for approx. 40mins, or until the bolognese has reduced 
	and thickened

	7. Serve with cooked pasta of your choice and garlic bread - yum!
